🌮 Spicy Tacos

Prep Time: 15 mins    Cook Time: 10 mins    Serves: 2

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 300g ground beef or plant-based meat
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Hot sauce (to taste)

👨‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef with taco seasoning for 7–10 minutes.
  2. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  3. Assemble tacos: add meat, lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and hot sauce.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy your spicy tacos!
