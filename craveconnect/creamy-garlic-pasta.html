🍝 Creamy Garlic Pasta

Prep Time: 10 mins    Cook Time: 20 mins    Serves: 2–3

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

👨‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and butter. Sauté garlic for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream, reduce heat, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce thickens.
  5. Add pasta to the sauce and toss well. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve hot!
