Prep Time: 10 mins    Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. Drizzle lime juice and olive oil over the mixture.
  3. Gently toss to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve fresh and enjoy!
