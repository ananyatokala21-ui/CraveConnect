  • 1 tsp garam masala

  • Salt to taste

Method ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ³

  1. In a bowl, mix curd, spices, lemon juice, and oil to make a thick marinade.
  2. Add paneer, capsicum, and onion cubes. Coat well and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer the marinated pieces and grill or bake until golden brown.
  4. Brush with butter for extra flavor and serve hot with mint chutney!
