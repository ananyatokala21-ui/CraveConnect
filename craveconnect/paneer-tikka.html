Paneer Tikka

🔥 Grilled paneer cubes marinated in aromatic Indian spices.

Ingredients 🧂

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Capsicum & onion cubes for skewering

Method 👩‍🍳

  1. In a bowl, mix curd, spices, lemon juice, and oil to make a thick marinade.
  2. Add paneer, capsicum, and onion cubes. Coat well and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer the marinated pieces and grill or bake until golden brown.
  4. Brush with butter for extra flavor and serve hot with mint chutney!
